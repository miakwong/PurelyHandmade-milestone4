Our Story

A journey of passion, craftsmanship, and dedication to handmade art.

Discover how Purely Homemade began and the values that drive us every day.

Our Beginning

Purely Homemade started with a simple idea: to celebrate and share the beauty of handcrafted items in a world increasingly dominated by mass production.

The Idea Takes Shape

January 2018

Our founder, Sarah Johnson, was inspired to create a platform that would connect artisans with appreciative customers after visiting local craft markets around the world. She saw incredible talent that deserved a wider audience.

Our First Collection

March 2019

With just five artisans and a small selection of ceramic pieces, we launched our first collection. The response was overwhelming, selling out within weeks and confirming our belief in the market for quality handcrafted goods.

Growing Our Team

June 2020

As demand grew, we expanded our team and network of artisans. We brought on Emma Thompson (ceramics), Michael Chen (woodworking), and Sophia Williams (textiles) as our first featured designers, each bringing their unique expertise.

Going Online

February 2021

We launched our e-commerce platform, making our collections available internationally. This allowed us to connect artisans with customers worldwide while maintaining our commitment to quality and authenticity.

Today & Beyond

Present Day

Purely Homemade now represents over 50 artisans across multiple disciplines. We continue to grow while staying true to our founding principles: supporting craftspeople, promoting sustainable practices, and bringing beautiful handmade items to homes everywhere.

Our Values

Craftsmanship

We believe in the value of items made by hand with care and skill. Each piece tells a story of dedication and passion that cannot be replicated by machines.

Sustainability

Our artisans work with sustainable materials and environmentally friendly practices. We believe in creating beautiful objects that don't compromise our planet's future.

Community

We foster a community that connects makers and appreciators. By supporting artisans directly, we help sustain traditional crafts and local economies.

Transparency

We believe in being open about how our products are made, who makes them, and how the revenue is shared. Every purchase directly supports an artisan's livelihood.

Quality

We curate our collections carefully, selecting pieces that meet our high standards for materials, design, and durability. We stand behind everything we sell.

Innovation

While we honor traditional techniques, we also celebrate innovation in craft. We encourage our artisans to experiment and push boundaries in their work.
