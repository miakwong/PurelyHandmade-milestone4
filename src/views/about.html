Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To preserve and promote the tradition of handmade craftsmanship by creating a sustainable marketplace that fairly compensates artisans while connecting them with appreciative customers worldwide.

We strive to make handmade goods accessible and valued in a world dominated by mass production, helping artisans earn a living wage for their skilled work.

Our Vision

A world where handmade craftsmanship is valued and preserved, with artisans able to sustain their practices and pass traditions to future generations.

We envision a marketplace where consumers understand the value of handcrafted items, appreciate the stories behind them, and choose quality, meaning, and sustainability over mass-produced alternatives.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all your products really handmade?

Yes, absolutely. Every item we sell is 100% handcrafted by our network of artisans. We personally verify the production process for each product to ensure authenticity.

How do you ensure fair compensation for artisans?

We work directly with our artisans to establish fair pricing that reflects their skill, time, and materials. On average, our artisans receive 60-70% of the final selling price, compared to the industry standard of 10-15%.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 30 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary depending on the destination. You can view specific shipping information during checkout.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items in their original condition. Due to the handmade nature of our products, there may be slight variations in color, texture, or size, which are not considered defects.

How can I become a Purely Homemade artisan?

We're always looking for talented craftspeople to join our community. Please visit our "Become an Artisan" page to learn about our selection criteria and application process.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer eco-friendly gift wrapping using handmade paper and natural materials for a small additional fee. You can select this option during checkout.
